Becoming a member of Phi Mu Delta means becoming a part of something greater than yourself. Joining our organization means committing to becoming a better man. It’s about joining a group of individuals; gentlemen who uphold the values of Democracy, Service to the community and Brotherhood toward their fellow man. Through our values and the friendship we provide one can expect to gain the means to better themselves and those around them.
