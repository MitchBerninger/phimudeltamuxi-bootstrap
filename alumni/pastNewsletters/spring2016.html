Dear Alumni,

Our fraternity has been very busy over the last year. This past Fall semester we volunteered at a 5K Color Run, sold teddy bears to raise money for St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, and attended ‘Safe Zone’ training. This semester we participated in the MLK Peace Walk, painted fingernails for MAN-I-Cures for a Cause, and plan to attend a Camp Victory clean-up day towards the end of April.
